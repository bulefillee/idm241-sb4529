Alpha Description


Micro-interaction:

Link -

click me

or https://store.ubisoft.com/us/home/?lang=en_US


Triggers -

User-initiated micro-interaction is triggered by hovering over the game card.

Rules -

  • Cursor changes to pointer within the bounds of the game game card.

  • Game card slides up, and the game card image has a darker overlay(opacity about 75% or more) appear with 2 icons : “Windows” icon at the bottom Center & the “Add to Wishlist” icon at the top right.

  • Hovering over the “Add to Wishlist” icon, the new icon with the filled in green heart & white background slides down as replacement, while a text box with “ Add to Wishlist” appears as a mode.

Feedback -

  • Cursor changes to pointer within the bounds of the game game card.

  • Game card slides up, and the game card image has a darker overlay(opacity about 75% or more) appear with 2 icons : “Windows” icon at the bottom Center & the “Add to Wishlist” icon at the top right.

  • Hovering over the “Add to Wishlist” icon, the new icon with the filled in green heart & white background slides down as replacement, while a text box with “ Add to Wishlist” appears as a mode.

Loops & Modes -

No loops, and when the filled in green heart & white background slides down as replacement, the text box with “ Add to Wishlist” appears as a mode.
